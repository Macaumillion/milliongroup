Brand Story

RuiChenDelights' brand story stems from a deep appreciation for Taiwanese street food culture. Inspired by the HungRuiChen sandwich brand, Million Group Macau decided to bring more than just sandwiches to Macau - creating Asia's first RuiChenDelights tea house, allowing more citizens and tourists to taste authentic Taiwanese flavors. Beyond the classic sandwiches, Taiwan has many unique delicacies worth sharing with everyone.

RuiChenDelights - Born from Homesickness

The founder of RuiChenDelights traveled through Taiwan's streets and alleys to obtain traditional braised recipes from military village grandmothers,
incorporating them into RuiChenDelights' Taiwanese cuisine,
developing beef noodle soup and braised pork rice that combine Taiwanese and Macau flavors

Slow-Cooked with Premium Ingredients

RuiChenDelights' herbal soup contains 20 types of Chinese herbs, using premium ingredients with rich broth,
prepared using gentle heat for over 10 hours,
preserving the nourishing essence of Taiwanese cuisine
