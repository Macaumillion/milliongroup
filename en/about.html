About Million Group

Company Profile

Million Group is a diversified business group in Macau, primarily engaged in commercial property investment and management, brand agency, and food & beverage operations.

With years of experience in the Macau market, we have established a strong reputation and extensive business network. Our business covers commercial property investment and management, brand agency, and food & beverage operations, providing customers with high-quality products and services.

Our Founders

Mike Choi

Chairman

With over 20 years of experience in the Macau business community, Mike Choi has led Million Group to become one of the leading business groups in Macau. His vision and leadership have been instrumental in the group's success.
