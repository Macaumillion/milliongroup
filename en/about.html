About Million Group

Founded in June 2012 and headquartered in Macau Peninsula, Million Group is committed to "building a diverse, innovative, and excellent business ecosystem," providing efficient and innovative business solutions for Macau and the Greater Bay Area. With robust strategies and market insights, the group has rapidly expanded its presence, becoming a trusted enterprise group in the Greater Bay Area.

Currently, Million Group owns fifteen subsidiaries, including creative industrial building revitalization, shared office spaces and commercial venue management, local and overseas real estate transactions and leasing, brand retail agency, multimedia marketing, renovation engineering, and interior design. The group possesses professional service capabilities and resource integration abilities.

Guided by the principles of professionalism, dedication, integrity, and positivity, Million Group serves customers with efficiency and innovation, becoming a trusted partner in the industry and the preferred choice in the hearts of Macau customers.

Ryan Hoi

Mike Choi and Ryan Hoi are the Executive Directors and Co-founders of Millionaire Group. In 2012, they jointly invested millions of Hong Kong dollars to establish Billionaire Property Limited. With their extensive market experience, professional sales skills, unique thinking, and eloquence, they successfully penetrated the local market for residential and commercial properties in Macau, and later expanded to overseas and the Greater Bay Area real estate markets in China. In 2024, Millionaire Group achieved a total property transaction value of several billion Hong Kong dollars, significantly driving the growth of the Asian real estate industry.

Both Mr. Choi and Mr. Hoi possess exceptional business acumen and analytical abilities, allowing them to keenly identify market trends and opportunities. They excel in integrating industry resources in Macau and have in-depth understanding of the Greater Bay Area market ecosystem. Furthermore, during the pandemic, they demonstrated their precise vision and business models by renovating multiple aging shopping malls in Macau and introducing new commercial formats, successfully creating new consumption destinations on the city's map.

As leaders, they navigate the group's development with wisdom and insights, showcasing remarkable innovative thinking and execution capabilities. Their meticulous planning and professional execution have enabled them to expand into local and international markets, achieving outstanding performance in Macau, the Greater Bay Area, and other regions. Mr. Choi and Mr. Hoi are frequently invited to participate in events organized by national enterprises, casinos, and banks in the Greater Bay Area, further solidifying their understanding of the market and providing more development opportunities. As a result, Millionaire Group has established itself as a leading brand consultant and real estate developer in Macau and the Greater Bay Area.
