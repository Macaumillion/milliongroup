Brand Story

HungRuiChen is a renowned Taiwanese sandwich brand with over 70 years of history. Known for its signature three-layer sandwiches, the brand has become a household name across Asia. In Macau, HungRuiChen is exclusively distributed by Million Group, bringing the authentic taste of Taiwan to local consumers.
